Talk to Us

The first and only student run multi-disciplinary lab of SRM University. The lab has a funding of $5K+ from the University and has currently 70 members and associates with it. Students from 8 departments work in 5 research groups concentrating on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, IoT, Computational Biology, Electrical Systems and Mixed Reality(AR/VR).
The lab follows no hierarchical structure and fosters student ideas and interests. Next Tech Lab has advisors from around the world from distinguished affiliates and alumni of institutions like MIT, University of Pittsburgh etc. The lab is currently working on 21 different projects.
